Metacharacters:
^ - starts with 
$ - ends with 
[ ] - group the chars
[pqrs] - match anything p/q/r/s
{m,n} - min of m length and max of n length 
{1,10}

{,n} - min of anything but max of n 
{,5} - 0 1 2 3 4 5 
(m,} - min of m and max of anything 
{1,} - 1 2 3 4 5 ......
{m} - exactly the m length 
(this|that) - or 
* - 0/more occurence of prev char 
a* - a/aaaaaa
.- exactly 1 
a. - aa
+ - 1/more occurence of prev char 
a+ - aa/aaaaa
? - optional

2.ERE - Extended Regular Expression 
egrep/grep -E
3.PRE - Perl Regular Expression
grep -P
